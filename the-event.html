Jesse and Monica will be married on Saturday, September 20th, 2014 at the Ebell of Los Angeles.

The Ceremony will commence at 5:00pm in the courtyard of the Ebell. In an attempt to represent the 3 cultures Monica and Jesse were raised with, the ceremony will be a fusion of aspects of American, Jewish, and Persian wedding traditions.

The Reception will be hosted adjacent to the courtyard in the Ebell's dining room immediately following the ceremony. Dinner and drinks will be served with live music and vivacious dancing.

The Ebell of Los Angeles is located at 743 S. Lucerne Boulevard, Los Angeles, California. The Ebell was constructed in 1927 to house the clubhouse for the Ebell Women's Club and the renowned Wilshire Ebell Theatre. The building was declared a Cultural Historic Monument in 1982. Notable performances at the Ebell include Judy Garland's first audition and Amelia Aerhart's final public appearance before her around-the-world flight.
