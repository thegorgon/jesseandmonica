The Ceremony will commence at 5:00pm at the Ebell. In an attempt to represent the 3 cultures Monica and Jesse were raised with, the ceremony will be a fusion of aspects of American, Jewish, and Persian wedding traditions.

The Reception will be hosted at the Ebell immediately following the ceremony. Dinner and drinks will be served with live music and vivacious dancing.

The Ebell of Los Angeles is located at 743 S. Lucerne Boulevard, Los Angeles, California. The Ebell was constructed in 1927 to house the Ebell Women's Club and the renowned Wilshire Ebell Theatre. The building was declared a Cultural Historic Monument in 1982. Notable performances at the Ebell include Judy Garland's first audition and Amelia Aerhart's final public appearance before her around-the-world flight.
