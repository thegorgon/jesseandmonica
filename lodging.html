There are two options for lodging near the event location:

  • The Loews of Hollywood Hotel
  • The Park Plaza Hotel

✮ The Loews of Hollywood Hotel:

Reserve online or via phone at  1 (855) 563-9749 by asking for the Lienke/Reiss wedding block.

We will be staying at the The Loews Hollywood Hotel and have reserved a block of rooms at a group rate. The hotel is located in the heart of Hollywood with plenty to see and do within walking distance and a shuttle will be provided to and from the Ebell on Saturday. If you bring a car, note that the parking options are Valet is $40 per day or self-parking in the Hollywood-Highland complex at $13 per entry with no ins-and-outs.

The Loews is located at  1755 N Highland Ave, Hollywood, CA 90028

✮ The Park Plaza Hotel:

Reserve online or via phone at  1 (323) 931-1501 by asking for the Lienke/Reiss wedding block.

A nice little casual lodge, the Park Plaza is located just a few blocks from the Grove and two miles from the Ebell. Parking is free.

The Park Plaza is located at  6001 W 3rd St, Los Angeles, CA 90036
